Bye, Aestella

I knew it from the beginning. That I'm special
There might be people who are uncomfortable with that, but you can't please everyone
So, I couldn't go to school continuously
After many twists and turns, I and Estella met Jasper and Horace like fate

Using my outstanding fashion sense, I swept the streets
of London with perfect disguise and quick hands
When I was tired of stealing, I went into the Liberty Department Store I dreamed of
I wandered around the streets, but my passion for fashion was always sincere
But what is this? Cleaning the floor all day without touching the clothes?

When I was limited in patience, a baroness who holds onto London's fashion world appeared
Genius recognizes genius! I became a brand designer for Baroness
The moment I felt like I was going to achieve my dream for a while
I didn't know that the baron's wife was such a person...
So I decided to show you who I am
Bye, Aestella. I'm a croela now!
